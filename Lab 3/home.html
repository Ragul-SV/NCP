Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a NAAC accredited A grade, multi-campus, multi-disciplinary teaching and research institution. Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham was founded by the world-renowned humanitarian leader, Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi, Amma. As Chancellor of the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amma continues to guide the institution's mission and growth.
